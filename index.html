About Me

Hello! I’m John Paul Ariate from the Philippines, a passionate frontend developer and multimedia designer. I love turning creative ideas into fully functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly web applications.

I have hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, and I continuously explore modern frameworks and tools to enhance my coding craft. On the multimedia side, I have knowledged in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro, which allows me to create engaging graphics, animations, and video content.

I bring a mix of soft skills such as problem-solving, teamwork, creativity, and attention to detail, ensuring that my projects not only function well but also provide excellent user experiences.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring design trends, learning new programming technologies, experimenting with animations, and creating personal multimedia projects. My goal is to grow as a versatile developer-designer, contributing to meaningful projects while continuously improving my skills in both frontend development and multimedia design.
